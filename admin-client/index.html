CREATE APPOINTMENTS

When you click the button above the following happens:

  • The settings you created are fetched from the database, this includes blocked dates/days of the week.
  • Next, the algorithm uses all of this information to create your appointment availability. For now, your appointments are spaced out by 30 minute intervals. This may change in the future as if it is a requested enough feature I will come back and add a custom interval property to the general settings.
  • Your appointment slots will then be inserted and saved into a database. I have used a no sql database, specifically mongoDb. Don't worry about this if you are not a tech person, just know it is very efficient, fast, and I can expand it's abilities in the future should requests come in!
  • That's it! Now you have successfully stored all of your appointment slots that will be utilized by your website to dynamically assign your companies availability throughout your working days!

Helpful Notes

I created this scheduling software to include all the information you will need to automate the mapping of your availability, and the booking of your services. I have included some useful notes below to help you understand how all of the configuration settings work. Let me begin my giving a general break-down of how the software works:

General Breakdown

When a potential customer loads your website this software will immediately generate your appointment availability based on the settings that you have configured above. Your appointment slots are spaced out by 30 minutes. When a customer books an appointment slot (finishes checking out) that slot and all slots that are inside of the range of time your booked appointment will last will have it's property called 'Teams Available' be decremented by one. This is useful as it allows your business to grow and expand your availability on demand. If you are booking appointments for yourself, meaning you have no employees that are handling the services, you would want your 'Teams Available' property to be set to "1". In this scenario when an appointment gets booked the appointment slot itself and all appointment slots inside of the range of time for that booked appointment will be erased completely. This is because your 'Teams Available' property dropped to zero.

How to Define When Appt's Start/Stop

You define your availability with a start hour and last hour. This defines the first available appointment of your business day and the last available appointment of your business day respectively.

What is Travel Buffer?

Value in Minutes A 'Travel Buffer' allows a mobile business to allow for traffic and other unforeseen circumstances that are a reality of the industry. Set this property in minutes to add extra padding in-between booked appointments and available appointment slots that follow. If your business is not mobile you can set the 'Travel Buffer' to 0, or even just use it as a break time in-between appointment bookings.

Blocking Days Indefinitely

Value is a day of the week This setting is for blocking a day of the week for the on going future, until you decide to restore it. An example use-case would be if you would like to have weekends off for your business you would use this setting to block saturday and sunday.

Blocking Dates

Value is a date (Year / Month / Day) This setting is for blocking a specific date, such as a holiday or maybe a vacation for your business. Your appointments are generated for 2 years out whenever a user loads your website, therefore I recommend blocking all the important holidays you wish to be closed on for at least the next 3 years. This means you should block Christmas and Christmas Eve for the next 3 years assuming you want those dates off. I did not pre-set any blocked dates, as to allow you to fully customize your availability.
